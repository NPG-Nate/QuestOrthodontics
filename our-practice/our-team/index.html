Meet Our Team

The Quest Orthodontics Team

Whether you are visiting our Atlanta office, or have a quick question about starting treatment, our highly experienced team is here to make your smile journey as comfortable and pleasant as possible.

At Quest Orthodontics, our friendly and accommodating team of orthodontic professionals in Sandy Springs and Decatur, Georgia, is committed to providing each and every patient with the quality service they deserve. Our team takes the time to talk with patients and truly get to know them. We are also incredibly experienced and helpful with insurance. We want your experience with us to be as rewarding and comfortable as possible, and we encourage you to contact us today to schedule an appointment with our talented orthodontist, Dr. Arjun Patel!
